About BirdieBuzz

BirdieBuzz is a lightweight, AI-driven SMS notification tool that brings live golf action straight to your phone. Whether you're following a friend in a local tournament or a rising star, we deliver hole-by-hole updates without needing to refresh leaderboards.

Our mission is to make golf spectating more personal and instant — so you never miss a birdie, eagle, or bogey from your favorite players. It's all signal, no noise.

Built by golf fans, for golf fans.
